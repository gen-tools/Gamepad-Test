What is Gamepad Test?

The Gamepad Tester is an online diagnostic tool that scans your controller in real-time. Check buttons, analog sticks, triggers, and vibration feedback to identify stick drift, unresponsive inputs, or faulty vibration motors. Get precise results instantly and know exactly what’s wrong with your controller.

How to Use Gamepad Test

Using Gamepad Test is fast and simple:

  • Connect your device (Gamepad, GPU, mic, or MIDI).
  • Select the appropriate tester.
  • Follow on-screen prompts to run diagnostics.
  • View real-time results and troubleshoot hardware issues immediately.

No downloads, no complicated setups—just instant diagnostics.
